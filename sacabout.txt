What each part does — simply
Actor — decides what action to take. Outputs a mean and std to sample actions from. This gives exploration naturally without epsilon-greedy.
Critic — evaluates how good an action is. Has two Q networks (double Q-learning) to avoid overestimation — same as the paper.
train() — three updates every step:

Critic → learns better Q values using TD error
Actor → learns to take actions that maximize Q
Alpha → automatically tunes how much to explore

reset() — ⭐ wipes all three networks (actor, critic, target). Buffer is untouched — it lives outside.
